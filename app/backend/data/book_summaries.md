## Title: 1984
A dystopian story about a totalitarian society controlled by surveillance, propaganda, and thought police. Winston Smith secretly rebels against the system in search of truth and freedom. Themes include control, manipulation, and individual resistance.

## Title: The Hobbit
Bilbo Baggins, a quiet hobbit, embarks on an unexpected journey with a group of dwarves to reclaim their treasure from the dragon Smaug. Along the way, he discovers courage and friendship. Themes include adventure, friendship, and self-discovery.

## Title: Harry Potter and the Sorcerer’s Stone
Harry discovers he is a wizard and begins his studies at Hogwarts, where he makes friends and faces dark forces. The story follows his growth and bravery. Themes include magic, friendship, and destiny.

## Title: The Lord of the Rings
Frodo sets out on a dangerous quest to destroy the One Ring and save Middle-earth from darkness. It is an epic tale of sacrifice and loyalty. Themes include friendship, heroism, and good versus evil.

## Title: To Kill a Mockingbird
The story follows a lawyer defending an innocent man in a racially divided society, seen through the eyes of his children. It explores justice and empathy. Themes include morality, justice, and prejudice.

## Title: The Great Gatsby
Jay Gatsby builds a life of wealth and illusion to win back his lost love. The story explores the American Dream and its emptiness. Themes include love, ambition, and illusion.

## Title: Brave New World
A seemingly perfect society where people are controlled through pleasure and conditioning. An outsider begins to question the system. Themes include control, freedom, and technology.

## Title: The Catcher in the Rye
Holden Caulfield shares his experiences and struggles with the adult world. It is a story about identity and alienation. Themes include growing up, loneliness, and authenticity.

## Title: Animal Farm
An allegorical tale about farm animals who rebel against humans but end up under a new tyranny. It reflects the corruption of power. Themes include politics, manipulation, and power.

## Title: Dune
Paul Atreides rises to power on a desert planet where valuable resources shape politics and survival. The story blends science fiction with philosophy. Themes include power, destiny, and survival.

## Title: The Alchemist
A young shepherd travels in search of treasure and discovers deeper meaning in life. His journey teaches him about following dreams. Themes include destiny, spirituality, and purpose.

## Title: The Book Thief
Set in Nazi Germany, a young girl finds comfort in stealing books and sharing stories. The narrative highlights the impact of war on ordinary people. Themes include war, humanity, and the power of words.

## Title: The Hunger Games
Katniss is forced to participate in a deadly competition controlled by an oppressive regime. She becomes a symbol of resistance. Themes include survival, rebellion, and inequality.

## Title: Pride and Prejudice
Elizabeth Bennet navigates love and social expectations in a rigid society. The story explores personal growth and misunderstandings. Themes include love, class, and judgment.

## Title: The Maze Runner
A group of boys wake up in a mysterious maze with no memory of their past and must find a way out. The story is filled with suspense and teamwork. Themes include survival, mystery, and cooperation.

## Title: The Fault in Our Stars
Two teenagers with cancer fall in love and explore life together despite their illness. It is an emotional journey about living fully. Themes include love, mortality, and meaning.

## Title: Ender’s Game
A gifted child is trained to lead humanity in a war against alien forces. He struggles with the morality of his actions. Themes include strategy, ethics, and responsibility.

## Title: The Chronicles of Narnia
Children discover a magical world where they must help restore peace and defeat evil forces. It is a classic fantasy adventure. Themes include good versus evil, courage, and faith.

## Title: The Shining
A writer moves with his family to an isolated hotel where supernatural forces begin to influence his mind. Tension builds as reality blurs. Themes include isolation, madness, and fear.

## Title: Dracula
The story follows a group of people trying to stop a powerful vampire threatening society. It is a gothic tale of horror and suspense. Themes include fear, the supernatural, and good versus evil.
